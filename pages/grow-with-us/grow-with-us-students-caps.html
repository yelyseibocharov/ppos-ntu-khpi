In order to represent and protect the rights and interests of students, the Primary Trade Union Organization of Students of Vinnytsia National Technical University (hereinafter referred to as the PUSVTU), which is a member of the Trade Union of Education and Science of Ukraine, was created and operates. The Primary Trade Union Organization of Students is a voluntary non-profit public organization of students and postgraduates of the University, whose purpose is to represent, exercise and protect.
